What is Amazement Park?

This is a park for the whole family to enjoy. In this amazing park there are multiple different rides that the whole family can enjoy. From the tallest ride to the fastest ride in Europe. Our park ranges in rides for people which like horror to people which like the thrill. Amazement park has a childrens area which allow the children, which are not quite tall enough to get on to the rides, to have fun. This area has other children and members of staff which will supervise the children making sure that your children are safe. With quality food and a great themed hotel suited to you and your families liking your whole family can enjoy a holiday at Amazement Park.
