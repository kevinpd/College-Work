Elephants

These elephants are endangered and is one of our first animals that we brought in to the zoo. We currently have 5 adult elephants and 3 baby elephants being fed and taken care of. This is an animal that can become very voilent if harrassed or abused. However the baby Elephants have become very friendly and trustworthy to the zookeepers. With this we will soon be opening a playground for the children and adults that would like to play with the Elephants. However our number one priority is to keep them safe so we do not allow visitors to feed them.

Tigers

We are currently raising only 3 Tigers and have 1 cub These tigers are also extinct and we are trying our best to keep them from dying out. This breed of tiger is extremely voilent therefore giving it a hard time to raise them however we are doing our best and doing quite well. The current cub we have has been bred and raised in captivity and has been handled with care by humans, making the cub comfortable with humans approaching her.

African Penguin

African Penguins are found closer south to Africa and are slowly dying out. We have brought them to our zoo and have put them in a large enclosure with lots of water and where the climate is similar to the sourthern Africa. We have 20 Penguins currently in our zoo and are drastically getting more.
