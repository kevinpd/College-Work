Who are we

We are an organisation that is trying to make people aware of the amount of endangered animals in the wild. We are providing these animals with a habitat that is not a danger to them. Our team is soley trying to raise the population of these animals. The New Zoo is trying to achieve these goals and we need your help so come and visit our zoo and spread the word.

Help our cause

There are many different ways in which you can help reach our goal. From the simplest of things like telling other people of our goal to donating money to us. We are currently making merchandise that will be coming soon. You are probably thinking why you should even help us in the first place, well we are an organisation that helps endangered animals so if you are an animal lover sparing a few coins would go a long way for the animals.
