Welcome to The New Zoo

The New Zoo is an upcoming zoo being built in Utah, Zion national Park. Our goal is to bring population to the endangered animals but allowing them to feel as free as possible. Our zoo provides our animals with a large open enclosure with a feeding system that allows them to feel like they are hunting in the wild. Some of our animals have been raised in the zoo and have become very familiar with humans allowing visitors to pet and feed with the animals.
